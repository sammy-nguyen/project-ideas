Pitch/idea: E-commerce shopping website

- Users can search the product
- Users can add products into shopping cart ( the cart item on the upper right can show the number of products)
- Users can delete products
- Users can increase/decrease the quantity of products
- Users can see the final total after deleting/ increasing / decreasing
- Users can choose check out as a guest or create account
- Users can check out with card (includes card number, expire date and security code)
